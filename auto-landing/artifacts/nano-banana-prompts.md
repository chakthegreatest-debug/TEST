Инструмент Nano Banana недоступен из текущей среды Codex, поэтому ниже сохранены готовые промпты для генерации и последующей замены локальных SVG-демо-ассетов в `assets/images/`.

## `assets/images/hero-cars.png`

Use case: ads-marketing  
Asset type: landing page hero image for a car selection and import service  
Primary request: photorealistic premium scene with several modern cars, no visible brand badges or recognizable logos, no readable license plates  
Scene/backdrop: clean contemporary delivery studio/showroom with subtle dark navy and purple atmosphere, polished floor, large soft light panels, hint of international import logistics without text  
Subject: three to four modern cars: sedan, crossover, electric-looking SUV, positioned as a trusted curated selection  
Style/medium: high-end editorial automotive photography, realistic, not CGI-looking  
Composition/framing: wide 16:9 horizontal composition, cars on the right and center, clean dark calm area on the left suitable for landing page text overlay  
Lighting/mood: refined, confident, premium, soft reflections  
Color palette: dark navy, deep purple, white highlights, small lime-green accent light  
Constraints: no text, no watermark, no visible logos, no recognizable car brand badges, no people, no UI frames, no split layout cards
